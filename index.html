                          ABOUT US

                          It all began in the summer of 1989. While driving back from a summer vacation, Neil Rubenstein's two son's wanted to stop in Ann Arbor, MI so that they could get official sweatshirts and football jerseys from the University of Michigan. An hour later with $250 worth of newly acquired sweatshirts, t-shirts, shorts and jerseys packed into the trunk of the car the family returned to the road to finish the drive back to suburban Chicago. While on the drive, Neil thought to himself, "If my kids want this stuff so bad that I have to drive an hour out of our way, there have got to be more kids like mine and more parents willing to do the same." It was at that time that the idea for Campus Colors was born.

                          Campus Colors was started in 1990 with the premise of bringing authentic college bookstore merchandise to suburban Chicago. The idea was simple: rather than travel to your favorite college to get merchandise to support your favorite school, Campus Colors would bring the merchandise of the school to you. What started out as a small retail store free standing commercial building that resembled a fraternity house on a college campus, Campus Colors has grown into a family run chain with locations throughout the Chicagoland area. And now with the Pro Colors store, Campus Colors is able to offer all of your favorite pro team merchandise too.

                          Both inside our brick and mortar stores and now on-line, Campus Colors carries one of the largest selections of top quality, licensed collegiate sportswear and novelties in the country. Our merchandise is authentic-we buy from the same top companies that supply collegiate bookstores across the country, that's why we truly are "The Authentic Off Campus College Store." In addition, every single item in our stores is officially licensed, which means that the university or school has approved that item and a portion of the proceeds from the sale of that product go directly to that school. With merchandise representing over 200 colleges and universities you're sure to find something for collegiate fans of all ages.

                          Featuring top quality merchandise by vendors such as: Nike, Adidas, Reebok, Majestic, Top of the World, and more, Campus Colors offers a wide selection of sweatshirts, t-shirts, shorts, pants, hats, jerseys, and novelties for men, women, and children. Ranging in size from toddler to XXL we can outfit just about anyone.

                          With over 400 different hooded sweatshirts, 750 different t-shirts, 300 different pairs of shorts and over 1000 different novelties, our selection is unparalleled and unique. What's more, approximately 60% of all of our merchandise is custom designed to meet the high standards of Campus Colors' customers. By designing our own merchandise, we are able to deliver top quality, clean, timeless and fashionable designs that look great both now and for many years to come.

                          Returns and Exchanges

                          All returns must be unused and in new saleable condition with all original hangtags affixed and packaging intact. We reserve the right to refuse any returned merchandise if it is does not meet the previously mentioned conditions. Additionally, should Campus Colors determine that your return is not saleable, the item will be returned to you at your expense.

                          Campus Colors accepts returns of merchandise within 30 days of the date that the order has shipped. Tags must still be attached and the item must be new, unused and unwashed. We will refund the credit card used to make the original purchase or issue a store credit or exchange. Original shipping and handling charges are not refundable. If you exchange an item, you will be charged for shipping and handling on the new item. If the original order qualified for any promotions or discounts, your refund will be less those discounts if your return causes your order to become unqualified. If you would like to make an exchange or return, please follow the instructions below. Clearance items are not covered under this warranty. All sales are final on Clearance merchandise. Clearance merchandise is exchangeable for size only in the exact same item only while quantities last - no exceptions.

                          Campus Colors requires your merchandise to be received back by us prior to processing your refund. If you have not received your order and would like a refund, please contact our Customer Service Department for further assistance. All orders are shipped with delivery confirmations via reputable 3rd party carriers. If delivery confirmations show a status of delivered, we will be unable to offer a refund without return of the merchandise-NO EXCEPTIONS.

                          If you would like to make an exchange or return, please follow the instructions below.

                          Step 1. Call Campus Colors at 866-598-CAMPUS (2267) or e-mail us at service@campuscolors.com within 30 days of the date of shipment to receive a return authorization number. For quicker processing have your receipt number which is in the upper corner of your sale receipt. Please note that all returns must be postmarked within 10 days of the date that the RMA number is issued or they will be refused.

                          Step 2. After you contact our Customer Service department, we will e-mail you a Return Merchandise Authorization form. This form must be included with the item(s) being returned to us.

                          Step 3. Clearly mark the return authorization number on the outside of the package. Shipments returned without return authorization numbers will be refused. To make sure the item you are returning reaches us without damage or loss, make sure it is properly packaged or boxed. We recommend you ship your package back to us with a carrier that can track your package to prevent loss.

                          Step 4. We will do our best to exchange your purchase or refund your money within one week upon receiving your merchandise back. Be sure that the 'Ship To Address' for all product returns is clearly legible and ship to:

                          Campus Colors Attention: Returns 1586 Old Deerfield Road Highland Park, IL 60035

                          If you were shipped a defective item or item in error, please call us or e-mail our customer service department at service@campuscolors.com (make sure to include your name, telephone number address and order number(. We will contact you to return the merchandise to us. If you do not follow this procedure, we cannot refund shipping expense incurred by you to send back erroneous merchandise.

                          Special order and sale merchandise is FINAL SALE and may not be returned or exchanged.

                          SHIPPING INFORMATION

                          FREE Standard Shipping for orders $75.00 and over
                          $3.99 Flat Rate Shipping for orders $74.99 and under

                          *Free and Flat Rate shipping offers are only available in the continental United States and apply to standard ground shipping only. There may be an additional charge on oversized items. An additional P&H fee for these items will be displayed in your cart, so you'll know the cost before you place your order.

                          On a Deadline?

                          Campus Colors offers discounted Priority, 2nd-Day and 1-Day shipping options. See details on upgrade charges when you view your cart at checkout. Items that are too heavy or too large will be shipped Ground delivery. Packages may require a signature. Does not include Saturday delivery. For Saturday delivery please call customer service at 866-598-2267.

                          Alaska, Hawaii & US Territories

                          Orders to these addresses are subject to additional charges. Shipping total will be displayed on the cart page before checkout. Priority, 2nd-Day and 1-Day delivery not available.

                          PO APO & FPO Addresses

                          These orders are shipped using our standard shipping and handling charges.

                          Shipping confirmations along with tracking numbers are sent to the e-mail on the order, the evening the order leaves the facility. We recommend that customers choose a secure location when selecting a shipping address. Campus Colors will not be held responsible for packages that are confirmed delivered by our 3rd party carriers. Ship to addresses requiring a correction may incur an additional charge.

                          Most orders ship within 1 business day of being placed. Orders shipping via 1 Day and 2nd Day will take priority. In some rare cases, please allow up to 2-3 business days for order processing.

                          Campus Colors uses the United States Postal Service and UPS for shipping of all packages to our customers. While these are proven, reliable carriers, occasionally packages do not arrive at their destinations within the proper allotted in-transit timeframe. At this time, the only in-transit time guaranteed delivery services available are 1 Day and 2 Day Air Services. USPS Priority Mail and Ground service are not day guaranteed, but rather approximations of when your package should arrive at its destination. While we will make every effort possible to assist our customers in having their packages delivered in a proper timely manner, we do not accept responsibility for negligence by our 3rd party carriers.

                          All free shipping offers are shipped via standard ground service. Orders qualify for free shipping promotions based upon the amount of the order after all discounts have been taken and before any applicable sales tax is added.
